FEATURES

Built for fighters, not spreadsheets.

Whether you’re shadowboxing at home, smashing rounds on the heavy bag, or mixing in conditioning intervals, PunchPOW helps you stay organized, on time, and in the zone.

WORKOUT PLANNER

Create custom fight rounds

Build workouts with Round, Rest, and Get Ready segments. Adjust times, reorder segments, and save routines that match your style of training.

READY-MADE ROUTINES

Start fast with presets

Choose from pre-built workouts designed for bag work, shadowboxing, and conditioning so you can start training in seconds.

CLEAR TIMER

Easy to see, even mid-round

A bold, high-contrast timer makes it simple to see the current phase and time remaining. Big Screen mode turns your device into a gym-friendly display.

SIRI SUPPORT

Hands-friendly controls

Use Siri to start, pause, resume, or skip rounds without taking off your gloves. Perfect when your hands are wrapped and you don’t want to tap the screen.

FEEDBACK

Sound and haptic cues

Clear audio and haptic signals help you feel transitions between rounds and rest, so you can stay focused on your movement instead of the clock.

PREMIUM

Upgrade when you’re ready

Premium unlocks additional workouts and advanced planning options. You can still try the core experience first and upgrade from inside the app at any time.
